Our Story

How It All Began

♥

2021

The First Meeting

It was an ordinary autumn afternoon when James and Elena first crossed paths at a small bookshop in the city. A shared reach for the same book on the shelf — and from that quiet moment, something extraordinary quietly began.

2022

Falling Together

Late night walks, long conversations over coffee, and a thousand small moments that neither could imagine ending. They fell in love slowly, then all at once — the way only the most important things in life tend to happen.

2024

The Proposal

On a quiet evening under the stars, James got down on one knee and asked Elena to spend forever by his side. With happy tears and a joyful yes, they began writing the next chapter together.

2026

Forever Begins

And now, surrounded by the people they love most, James and Elena are ready to say "I do." We are so grateful you are here to celebrate the beginning of their forever.
